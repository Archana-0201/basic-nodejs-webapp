About KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology

KPR Institute of Engineering and Technology (KPRIET) is a leading autonomous institution located in Coimbatore, Tamil Nadu. Established with the vision of creating industry-ready professionals, KPRIET emphasizes innovation, sustainability, and global learning exposure.

The institute offers cutting-edge programs in Engineering, Technology, and Management, with strong research culture and international collaborations.
